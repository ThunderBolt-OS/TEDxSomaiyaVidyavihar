Jamie Shaw

Race-Car Driver

Ever thought of living the fast life like Lewis Hamilton and Sebastian Vettel? Or wanted to taste the thrill of an exhilarating Formula one race?
Fasten your seatbelts as Jamie Shaw, one of India's youngest motorsport athletes, tells us about making his dream a reality and representing India on the world stage! Find out all about what it's like to put the pedal to the floor in a professional race on TEDxSomaiya Shots Season 2!

Le Funky (Gabriel)

Graffiti Artist

Have you ever wanted to travel the world and fill up every corner with your art?
Meet Gabriel AKA Le Funky the immensely talented Graffiti & Tattoo artist from Portugal who lives that dream every day! Learn about the challenges faced by him in his daily life, how he perfected his craft & what it's like to make a living off your passion, only on TEDxSomaiya Shots!

Uday Kumar Sangesetti

Micro Artist

Imagine having a pencil tip as the canvas for your art?
Creating his own world on the tip of a pencil with his sharp eye and larger-than-life creativity, Uday Kumar Sangasett is a world record holding micro-artist! From Superman to Mother Teresa , there is nothing which Uday Kumar Sangasetti can't carve on a pencil tip!

Dr. Amit Samarth

Premier Ultra Cyclist

“Ride as much or as little, as long or as short as you feel. But RIDE”
Cherish each moment, and feel alive in every second. Cycling is a sport which inspires and liberates you. Speed which gives you ‘wings’ and relieves your stress. Dr. Amit Samarth, India's Premier Ultra Cyclist, has an immense passion for cycling and he is making our country proud. Catch him talk about his experiences only on TEDxSomaiya Shots!

Captain Radhika Menon

1st Woman Merchant Navy Captain

"No two days are same on the ship"
The tides can be rather unforgiving, even more so as a woman in the merchant navy in 1991, despite the hurdles that one could face, Captain Radhika Menon made the country proud when she rescued seven fishermen who were stranded at sea. Watch her share her experience and more on TEDxSomaiya Shots!

Nishchay Luthra

Figure Skater

Nishchay Luthra is a nine time national award and three time international award winning figure skater, with dreams of representing India at the Winter Olympics!

Ayla Tesler-Mabe

Musician

A 20-year-old musician from Canada, she has amassed an enormous fan following courtesy of her spectacular guitar playing & her guitar lessons on YouTube. She's also a part of an amazing upcoming band named Ludic. Their tracks "My love" & "Heart Emoji" are a rage amongst their fans and with all these achievements, Ayla's journey towards success has just begun!
